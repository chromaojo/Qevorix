Privacy Policy

Your Information Matters

Information We Collect

Overview

Qevorix Compliance is committed to protecting client information and maintaining confidentiality across every interaction, submission, and communication process.

To provide regulatory compliance, documentation, advisory, and business support services effectively, Qevorix Compliance may collect personal, corporate, technical, and transactional information from clients, website visitors, and service users.

All information collected is handled in accordance with applicable Nigerian data protection laws, including the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and related regulatory frameworks.

Types of Information Collected

Personal Identification Information

We may collect information that identifies an individual directly or indirectly, including:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Residential or business address
  • Government-issued identification (where legally required)
  • Signature and authorization documents
Business and Corporate Information

For regulatory and compliance services, we may collect:

  • Company registration details
  • CAC documentation
  • Tax Identification Numbers (TIN)
  • Trademark documentation
  • Product registration details
  • Regulatory application documents
  • Import/export records
  • Packaging and label information
Financial and Transactional Information

Where applicable, we may collect:

  • Payment confirmation details
  • Billing information
  • Transaction references
  • Service subscription records

Qevorix does not store sensitive card or banking credentials directly on its servers unless processed through secured and compliant payment providers.

Technical and Usage Information

When users visit our website, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Website interaction data
  • Pages visited
  • Session duration
  • Referral sources
Communication Records

We may retain records of:

  • Emails
  • Consultation requests
  • Customer support interactions
  • WhatsApp or phone communications
  • Feedback submissions
  • Compliance inquiries

How Information Is Used

Purpose of Data Collection

Information collected by Qevorix Compliance is used strictly for legitimate business, regulatory, legal, and operational purposes.

Service Delivery and Compliance Processing
  • Process NAFDAC, SON, CAC, and related regulatory applications
  • Prepare and review compliance documentation
  • Facilitate communication with regulatory agencies
  • Verify business or client identity
  • Track project progress and approvals
  • Deliver consultation and advisory services
Client Communication
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Provide project updates
  • Share compliance requirements
  • Schedule consultations
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Provide customer support
Operational Improvement
  • Improve website performance
  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze service efficiency
  • Monitor system functionality
  • Detect technical issues or abuse
Legal and Regulatory Obligations
  • Comply with Nigerian laws and regulations
  • Respond to lawful government requests
  • Prevent fraud or unauthorized activity
  • Enforce contractual obligations
  • Protect legal rights and operational integrity

Data Protection Measures

Commitment to Data Security

Qevorix Compliance implements appropriate technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards to protect client information against unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, alteration, or loss.

Security Measures
  • Secure cloud-based storage systems
  • Access control and restricted authorization
  • Password-protected databases
  • Encrypted communication channels
  • Firewall and cybersecurity protections
  • Internal confidentiality procedures
  • Staff access limitations based on operational need
Employee and Internal Confidentiality

All employees, contractors, and third-party personnel handling client information are expected to maintain strict confidentiality obligations and comply with internal data protection standards.

Data Retention

Client information is retained only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill regulatory obligations
  • Complete service delivery
  • Resolve disputes
  • Maintain legal and accounting records
  • Comply with applicable laws
Limitation of Liability: While Qevorix Compliance adopts commercially reasonable security measures, no internet-based system or electronic transmission method is completely secure.
